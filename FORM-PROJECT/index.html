A Brief History

Sonic Began life in 1991 thanks to SEGA and his creators (Naoto Ohshima, Yuji Naka and Hirokazu Yasuhara are generally credited with the creation of Sonic).

Sonic is instantly recognisable with his blue hedgehog appearance, red-white and gold (Michael Jackson inspired) trainers, white gloves and character traits even when he appears in hundreds of video clips and games worldwide, either as a playable character, ai or in films and cartoons.
