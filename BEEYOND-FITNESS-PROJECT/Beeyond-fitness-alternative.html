A little bit about us.

Welcome!


First we would like to say a huge welcome, welcome to BEEYOND FITNESS, the home of everything fitness and health.

You may have heard about us from our ever growing number of members, seen our leaflets, come across our facebook page or even a google search. Where ever you are or whatever your fitness or health related goals were here to help you, advise you and guide you along your journey.

We started out in 2018 and have continued to grow since, even with the pandemic situation we've added more and more members and clients. We simply adapted and continued doing what we do best.

We were one of the first in the area to embrace the online virtual classes via Zoom, of which were still running aswell as getting back to some outdoor classes, regulations allowing and opening the studio back up once we get back to normality.

We have also grown to a team of three instructors, who have all had their own health and fitness journeys so we have a wealth of knowledge as well as personal experience to bring to the table.

We also have a Business page on Facebook which we update daily with news, business updates and any changes to timetables, motivational messages and we also have an exclusive group which is for members since as being part of the BEEYOD FITNESS family you can have your say and input on the content we run aswell as details on exclusive offers and competitions.

Links to our Facebook site and other Social media platforms can be found at the top of the page next to the menu and at the bottom of the page in the contact info section.

Lastly any queries or questions you may have, either post on the facebook site, or send us a message using the form at the bottom of the page.

We hope you choose us for your Health and Fitness Journey.
